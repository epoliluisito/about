Match rules
Arrows or WASD to move. Space is the only active button: it always kicks the ball in the direction the current player is facing, whether it is loose or at a player's feet. Hold time sets kick strength. There is no teammate targeting. When your goalkeeper has the ball, control switches to the goalkeeper so play never locks.

Restarts now follow football logic: throw-ins on the touchline, goal kicks when the attacking side last touched it behind the goal, corners when the defending side touched it out behind its own goal. Kick distance has been trimmed slightly from the previous build. Match clock is now a 90-second countdown.
